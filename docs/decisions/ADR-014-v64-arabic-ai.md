# ADR-014: Advanced Arabic AI Engine

## Status: Accepted
## Date: 2026-03-01

## Context
Platform requires semantic Arabic adaptation beyond simple translation, including sector-specific terminology, RTL visualization, and mixed-script stability.

## Decision
Dedicated Arabic AI Engine module with sector term maps, RTL axis reversal, mixed-script segmentation, and structural equivalence scoring. Hierarchy parity index ≥ 0.95 for Arabic transformation.

## Consequences
- Professional Arabic output with sector-appropriate terminology
- RTL visualizations maintain data distribution integrity
- Mixed Arabic+English content renders without baseline drift
