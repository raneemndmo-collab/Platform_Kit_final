# Incident Management (SRE-018)

| Severity | Definition | Response | Resolution |
|----------|-----------|----------|------------|
| P0 | Platform down / data loss | 15 min | 1 hour |
| P1 | Module down / SLO breach | 30 min | 4 hours |
| P2 | Feature degraded | 4 hours | 24 hours |
| P3 | Minor issue | Next day | 1 week |

Lifecycle: Detection → Triage → Response → Mitigation → Resolution → Review
